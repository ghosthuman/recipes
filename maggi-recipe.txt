Ingerdients:
1.Maggi Noodles (1 Packet)
2.Water (2 Cups)

Directions:
1. Pour 2 cups of water into a vessel. Heat it on gas, for 3 mins.
2. Put the maggi into the hot water.
3. Let the hot water loosen the noodles into separate strain. Use a fork to separate them.
4. Add maggi masala to the maggi.
5. Stir the contents so as to distrbute the masala evenly. Keep stiring till the colour becomes yellow evenly. Dont burn it though.
6. Pour the maggi noodles into a plate and serve (hopefully to yourself).
